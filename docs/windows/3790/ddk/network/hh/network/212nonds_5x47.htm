Power Management for WDM Lower Edge

NDIS handles all Plug and Play and power management IRPs on behalf of NDIS-WDM miniport drivers. Therefore, NDIS-WDM miniport drivers should respond to Plug and Play and power management OIDs, based on device capabilities, as described in Power Management for NDIS Miniport Drivers. For more information on these OIDs, see Plug and Play and Power Management OIDs.
