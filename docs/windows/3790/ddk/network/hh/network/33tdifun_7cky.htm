TDI Functions

This section describes system-supplied TdiXxx functions that are called by transport drivers or by their kernel-mode clients.

The functions described here are in alphabetic order. For quick reference, the following table summarizes which of these TdiXxx functions can be called by TDI transports, which can be called by their clients, and which can be called by both.

TdiXxx Function Called by
TdiCompleteRequest Transports
TdiCopyBufferToMdl Transports and clients
TdiCopyMdlToBuffer Transports and clients
TdiCopyMdlChainToMDLChain Transports and clients
TdiCopyLookaheadData NDIS protocol drivers (lower edge of transports)
TdiInitialize Transports
TdiMapUserRequest Transports
TdiMatchPdoWithChainedReceiveContext Transports
TdiReturnChainedReceives Clients
TdiRegisterPnPHandlers Clients of PnP-aware transports
TdiDeregisterPnPHandlers Clients of PnP-aware transports
TdiRegisterProvider PnP-aware transports
TdiProviderReady PnP-aware transports
TdiDeregisterProvider PnP-aware transports
TdiRegisterDeviceObject PnP-aware transports
TdiDeregisterDeviceObject PnP-aware transports
TdiRegisterNetAddress PnP-aware transports
TdiDeregisterNetAddress PnP-aware transports
TdiEnumerateAddresses Clients of PnP-aware transports
TdiPnPPowerRequest PnP-aware transports (and NDIS)
TdiPnPPowerComplete Clients of PnP-aware transports
