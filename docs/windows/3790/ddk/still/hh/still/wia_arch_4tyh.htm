Still Image Devices: Windows DDK

Device Properties

All still image devices have specific characteristics that describe that device. For example, two characteristics of a flatbed scanner are its horizontal and vertical bed sizes. A possible characteristic of a digital still camera is its battery status, which indicates remaining battery life.

Similarly, data produced or stored by the device can also be described with characteristics. For example, the data produced by a scanner can be described by the number of pixels on a line and the number of bits per pixel.

In WIA, characteristics describing both devices and data are referred to as properties. Properties exist in sets and are maintained by the WIA minidriver in a WIA driver item.
