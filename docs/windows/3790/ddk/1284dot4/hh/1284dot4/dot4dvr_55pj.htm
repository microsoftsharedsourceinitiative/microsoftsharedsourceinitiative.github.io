Dot4 Plug and Play Support

A Dot4 function handles the following Plug and Play requests:

IRP_MN_CANCEL_REMOVE_DEVICE

IRP_MN_CANCEL_STOP_DEVICE

IRP_MN_QUERY_BUS_INFORMATION

IRP_MN_QUERY_CAPABILITIES

IRP_MN_QUERY_DEVICE_RELATIONS

IRP_MN_QUERY_DEVICE_TEXT

IRP_MN_QUERY_ID

IRP_MN_QUERY_PNP_DEVICE_STATE

IRP_MN_QUERY_REMOVE_DEVICE

IRP_MN_QUERY_STOP_DEVICE

IRP_MN_REMOVE_DEVICE

IRP_MN_START_DEVICE

IRP_MN_STOP_DEVICE

IRP_MN_SURPRISE_REMOVAL

For all other Plug and Play requests, the Dot4 driver sets the Status member of the I/O status block to STATUS_SUCCESS, and completes the request without further processing.
