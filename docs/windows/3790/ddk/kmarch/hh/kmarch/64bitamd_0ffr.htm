Saved Registers for Caller and Callee

The registers RAX, RCX, RDX, R8, R9, R10, R11 are volatile and are, therefore, destroyed on function calls (unless analysis such as Whole Program Optimization can prove their safety).

The registers RBX, RBP, RDI, RSI, R12, R13, R14, and R15 are considered nonvolatile and must be saved and restored by a function that uses them.
