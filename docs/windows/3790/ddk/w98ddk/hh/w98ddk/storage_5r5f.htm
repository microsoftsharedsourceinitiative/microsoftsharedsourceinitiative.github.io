IOS_Register

VxDCall IOS_Register, <<OFFSET32 DRP>>

Registers an IOS client or driver. The function copies the addresses of the various IOS linkage services to the I/O linkage block (ILB) of the client or driver and initializes structures maintained internally by the IOS.

  • Sets the DRP_reg_result member of the DRP structure.
DRP
Address of a DRP structure that contains information for registering the driver or client.
