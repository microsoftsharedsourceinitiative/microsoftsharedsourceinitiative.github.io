Writing Windows 2000 Drivers

Information about writing drivers for Windows 2000 is provided in the documentation. Follow those recommendations for ensuring that you have up-to-date information for writing and testing display device drivers for Windows 2000:

  • Provide evaluation equipment for testing during Windows 2000 development.

    Be sure to also provide the complete specifications for the equipment, including component specifications. This greatly speeds the problem identification and resolution process. To provide systems to Microsoft for development with Windows 2000, please send e-mail to nt5req@microsoft.com. Please include your name, title, company name, company type (IHV, ISV, ISP, or OEM), and phone and fax numbers.

  • Submit new drivers to WHQL for testing.

    If you are developing new hardware or making changes that result in a new binary, be sure to submit your drivers, INF files, and hardware to WHQL for testing. For information, please send e-mail to whqlinfo@microsoft.com.
