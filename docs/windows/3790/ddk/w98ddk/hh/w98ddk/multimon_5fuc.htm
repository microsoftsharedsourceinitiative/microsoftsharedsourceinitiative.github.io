Adding Multimonitor Support

For users, the addition of multimonitor support in the operating system means more room for displaying applications. For developers who provide drivers for traditional graphics adapters and multiple output devices, multimonitor support means that new multimonitor-aware device drivers must be written for Windows 98/Me and Windows® 2000 and later.
