Volume Tracking and NEC Floppy Media Change Detection

No changeline support will be assumed until a changeline is actually detected by the system. Once a changeline has been detected, it will be honored for subsequent I/O request to the media. This will guarantee that Windows 95 continues to behave like MS-DOS 5 on older systems that do not support change line.
