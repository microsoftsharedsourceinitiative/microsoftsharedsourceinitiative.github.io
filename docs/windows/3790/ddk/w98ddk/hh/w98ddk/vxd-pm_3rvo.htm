CONFIG_APM_RESUME_CRITICAL and CONFIG_APM_RESUME_SUSPEND

The configuration manager sends the CONFIG_APM_RESUME_CRITICAL and CONFIG_APM_RESUME_SUSPEND messages to inform device drivers and bus drivers that the system has awakened.

The CONFIG_APM_RESUME_SUSPEND message is sent when the system is waking normally. CONFIG_APM _RESUME_CRITICAL is sent when the system is waking after an emergency sleep in which the drivers were not told the system was sleeping (for example, due to power failure).

Device drivers should do nothing and wait until the device needs to be turned on according to its power policy.

Bus drivers should put their buses in a power state that is high enough to detect device insertions and removals. This may require turning on the bus (with Set_DevNode_PowerState) and doing a re-enumeration.
