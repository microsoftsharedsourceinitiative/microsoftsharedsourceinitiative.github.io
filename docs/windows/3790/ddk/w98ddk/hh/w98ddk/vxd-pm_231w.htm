Device Power Management

Devices that are not in use should be put into low power states to conserve power. Under OnNow, the responsibility for managing power to devices is divided into three parts:

  • Policy Owners decide when a device should switch power states.
  • Device Drivers save and restore any hardware context lost by switching to a low power state.
  • Bus Drivers use standard interfaces to signal hardware to switch to a low power state and possibly remove power from the device. This includes the Advanced Configuration and Power Interface (ACPI) driver.
