Streaming States

Each stream provided by the driver exists in one of three states: stopped, paused, or running. Upon initialization, the stream is, by definition, in the Stopped state. Transitions to the other states are made through SRB_SET_STREAM_STATE.

State Description
KSSTATE_STOP Using absolute minimum of resources. No outstanding data SRBs at the driver.
KSSTATE_ACQUIRE Acquiring resources. This state does not have an analog in DirectShow.
KSSTATE_PAUSE Preparing to instantly make transition to the Run state. Allocating all needed resources such as bandwidth on USB and 1394.
KSSTATE_RUN Streaming. Fill buffers and complete SRBs through CompleteStreamSRB.
