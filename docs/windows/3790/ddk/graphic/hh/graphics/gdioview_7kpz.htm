GDI Service Routines

GDI exports many service routines, whose names have the form EngXxx. The driver dynamically links to win32k.sys to directly access these routines. GDI service routines include surface management, rendering simulations, and path, palette, font, and text services. These services are discussed in detail in GDI Support Services.
