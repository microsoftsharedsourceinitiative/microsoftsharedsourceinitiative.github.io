MIDI Port

The MIDI port driver provides MIDI functionality. The adapter driver provides a corresponding MIDI miniport driver object that binds itself to the MIDI port driver object to form a MIDI filter (see MIDI and DirectMusic Filters) that can capture or play back a MIDI stream. The port and miniport driver objects communicate with each other through their respective IPortMidi and IMiniportMidi interfaces.

In addition, PortCls contains built-in miniport drivers for MIDI devices that have FMSynth, DMusUART, and UART functions. For information on using these built-in miniport drivers, see PcNewMiniport.
