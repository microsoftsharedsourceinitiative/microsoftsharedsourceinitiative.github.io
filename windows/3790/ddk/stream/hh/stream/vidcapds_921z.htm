Streaming Devices (Video and Audio): Windows DDK

Flow of Control for Video Capture

The flow of control for video capture involves:

Initializing of the driver

Creating a Format

Opening and Closing a Stream

Determining or updating the Streaming States

Capturing Video from the stream

A video capture stream is used in this example. Non-video streams, such as VBI, behave similarly although they use different KS_DATARANGE_Xxx and KS_DATAFORMAT_Xxx structures.
