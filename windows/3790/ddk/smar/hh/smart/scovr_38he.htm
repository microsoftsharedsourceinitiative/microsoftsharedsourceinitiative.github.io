Management of IOCTL Requests in a Smart Card Reader Driver

Management of IOCTL requests is centered in the smart card driver library. For the most part, smart card reader drivers can simply pass IOCTL requests to the SmartcardDeviceControl (WDM) library routine.

However, the standard set of IOCTL requests serviced by the smart card driver library will not always be sufficient to fully support the capabilities of a reader device. Therefore, vendors might need to create their own IOCTLs. Furthermore, some of the standard IOCTL requests might require additional processing after being handled by the driver library. For both of these reasons, the driver architecture for smart card readers allows vendor-supplied reader drivers to implement a series of callback routines. These callback routines provide further processing of IOCTLs when needed.
