Writing an I2C Master MiniVDD

The miniVDD is the vendor-specific code that supports the display chip set. When the VGA chip set is the I2C master, this I2C support must be added to the miniVDD. The miniVDD exports two new entry points, I2C_OPEN and I2C_ACCESS. These entry points are called directly by the slave minidrivers and are exported by the miniVDD through the IRP_MN_QUERY_INTERFACE request.
