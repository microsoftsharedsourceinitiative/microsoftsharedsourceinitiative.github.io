ISDN Configuration Pages

As for any TAPI device, the properties for the ISDN line device are accessible through the specific TAPI app, through the TAPI lineConfigDialogEdit function. Properties are provided on a per-connection basis. (Note that a connection is associated with a particular line device.) In addition, vendors will be able to add their own pages to the line device properties.
