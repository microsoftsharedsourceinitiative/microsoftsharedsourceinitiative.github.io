POWER_STATUS

The POWER_STATUS structure is used to set and retrieve time functions

typedef struct _POWER_STATUS {
    BYTE   PS_AC_Line_Status;
    BYTE   PS_Battery_Status;
    BYTE   PS_Battery_Flag;
    BYTE   PS_Battery_Life_Percentage;
    WORD   PS_Battery_Life_Time;
}   POWER_STATUS;

Members

PS_AC_Line_Status
Indicates the state of the AC power input line.
PS_Battery_Status
Indicates the status of the battery.
PS_Battery_Flag
Indicates the presence of a battery and its charging state.
PS_Battery_Life_Percentage
Remaining expected life of the battery, in percent amount.
PS_Battery_Life_Time
Expected remaining life of the battery, in seconds.
