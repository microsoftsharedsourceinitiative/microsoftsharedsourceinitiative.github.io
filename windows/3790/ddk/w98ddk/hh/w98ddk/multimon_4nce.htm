VDD_OPEN

VDD_OPEN opens a device for a given name.

Entry Meaning
Client_EAX VDD_OPEN equate from Minivdd.inc or Minivdd.h.
Client_ES:EDI Device name.
Client_EDX Flag.

Exit Meaning
Client_EAX Handle of device that was opened. Also called the DEVNODE of this device, this is the value used in a VDD_CLOSE call to close the device. Also the value used in a VDD_GET_DISPLAY_CONFIG call.
VDD_OPEN Indicates that the call was unsuccessful or that an error occurred.
