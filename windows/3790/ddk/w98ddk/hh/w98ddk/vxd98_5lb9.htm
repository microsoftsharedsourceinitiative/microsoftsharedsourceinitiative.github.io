POWERTIME

The POWERTIME structure is used to set and retrieve time functions.

typedef struct _POWERTIME {
    WORD   Year;
    WORD   Month;
    WORD   DayOfWeek;
    WORD   Day;
    WORD   Hour;
    WORD   Minute;
    WORD   Second;
    WORD   Milliseconds;
} POWERTIME;

Members

Year
Indicates the year.
Month
Indicates the month, 1 (January) through 12 (December).
DayOfWeek
Indicates the day of the week, zero (Sunday) through 6 (Saturday).
Day
Indicates the day of the month, 1 through 31.
Hour
Indicates the hour of the day, zero through 23.
Minute
Indicates the minutes, zero through 59.
Second
Indicates the seconds, zero through 59.
Milliseconds
Indicates the milliseconds, zero through 999.
