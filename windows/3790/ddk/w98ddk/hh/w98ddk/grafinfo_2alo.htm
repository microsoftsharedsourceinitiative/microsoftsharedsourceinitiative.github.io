Maximum Gray Level

The wMaxGray box contains the maximum value that the Universal Printer Driver can pass as an argument in the printer's gray-fill command.

Diconix 150 Plus

The wMaxGray box is empty.

HP LaserJet IIP

For the HP LaserJet IIP printer, the maximum percentage of black pixels is 100. (This means that there are 100 black pixels and 0 white pixels in the maximum shade or fill pattern.)
