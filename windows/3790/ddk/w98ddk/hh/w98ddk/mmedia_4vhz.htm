MM_DRVM_OPEN

Notifies a video callback function or window that the video capture driver received a DVM_STREAM_INIT message.

  • No return value.
dwParam1, dwParam2
Not used.

This message is used by video capture drivers, installable video compressors, and other types of installable drivers whenever a device is opened.
