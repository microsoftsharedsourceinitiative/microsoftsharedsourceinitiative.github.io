VM_Terminate2

include vmm.inc

mov     ebx, VMHandle
mov     eax, VM_Terminate2
VMMCall System_Control

Similar to VM_Terminate, except that VM_Terminate2 is sent only to virtual devices marked version 4.0 and it is sent in reverse device initialization order. VM_Terminate2 is sent immediately after VM_Terminate.
