Scalable Font Information

The cbScaleFontDescriptor and cbScaleCharDescriptor controls specify the size, in bytes, of the font and character descriptors for a vector font. Because the Universal Printer Driver does not support downloading scalable fonts, these controls are disabled.

HP LaserJet IIP

The cbScaleFontDescriptor and cbScaleCharDescriptor boxes are empty.
