Windows Management Instrumentation

This documentation contains an introduction to Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI), a kernel-level instrumentation technology for the Microsoft® Windows® platform.

WMI provides the basis for hardware instrumentation in future Windows environments. Close coupling of WMI with services developed to conform to the Web-Based Enterprise Management (WBEM) initiative will allow Microsoft to simplify instrumentation and provide consistent, open access to management data.

Both WMI and WBEM are key components in Microsoft’s manageability and total cost of ownership containment efforts. WMI is integrated into both the Windows® 98 and Windows® 2000 kernel to provide driver data and events. WMI is an extensible design, and an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) or independent hardware vendor (IHV) can extend the instrumented data set.
