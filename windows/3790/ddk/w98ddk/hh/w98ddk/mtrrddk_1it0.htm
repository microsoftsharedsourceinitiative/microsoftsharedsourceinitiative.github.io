Enabling MTRR Support

MTRR support is the enabling of the Write Combining feature in the Intel Pentium Pro microprocessor. For both the Microsoft® Windows® 95 and Windows® 98 operating systems, this support is enabled by two function calls exported by Mtrr.vxd: MTRR_Get_Version and MTRRSetPhysicalCacheTypeRange.
