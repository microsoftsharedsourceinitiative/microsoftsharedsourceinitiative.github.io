INF Description

Display Driver Description for the Secondary Monitor

As in AT-compatible machines, the name of the driver and the miniVDD must be entered for the secondary display driver.

  • The secondary driver must be entered in drv2 entry as follows:

    HKR, DEFAULT, drv2, , [driver name]

  • The name of the miniVDD must be entered in the minivdd2 entry as follows.

    HKR, DEFAULT, minivdd2, , [miniVDD name]
