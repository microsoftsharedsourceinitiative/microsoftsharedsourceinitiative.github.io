VDD_GET_DISPLAY_CONFIG

VDD_GET_DISPLAY_CONFIG gets a DISPLAYINFO structure for a given device handle.

Entry Meaning
Client_EAX VDD_GET_DISPLAY_CONFIG equate from Minivdd.inc or Minivdd.h.
Client_EBX Device handle.
Client_ECX Size of the DISPLAYINFO structure.
Client_ES:EDI Points to place to store the DISPLAYINFO structure.
