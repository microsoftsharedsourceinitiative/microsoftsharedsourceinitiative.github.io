Installing Serial Devices

This section describes the requirements that are specific to installing serial devices.

For general information about installing devices, see Device Installation Overview.

Serenum Hardware IDs

Vendors should use the following hardware ID format to identify Plug and Play devices that are enumerated by Serenum:

Serenum\XxxxYyyy

In this format, Xxxx is a field of four ASCII characters specifying the EISA Manufacturing ID, and Yyyy is a field of four ASCII characters specifying the Product ID. These IDs are documented in the Plug and Play External COM Device Specification, Version 1.00, February 28, 1995 (see the parallel and serial technologies Web site).
