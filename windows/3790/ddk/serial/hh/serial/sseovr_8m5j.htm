Configuration of Serial Devices and Drivers

This section describes the following configurations of devices and drivers that Serial and Serenum support:

A non-Plug and Play serial device is connected to an RS-232 port.

A Plug and Play serial device is connected to an RS-232 port.

A Plug and Play serial device that requires a 16550 UART-compatible interface is not connected to an RS-232 port.

A Plug and Play serial device that requires a 16550 UART-compatible interface is one device on a multi-function device, and is not connected to an RS-232 port.
