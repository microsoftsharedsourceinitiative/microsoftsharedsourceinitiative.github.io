Allowed HAL Routines

WHQL restricts NDIS drivers from calling most HAL macros and routines. HAL macros and routines are implemented in Hal.dll.

Except for the following HAL macros, WHQL restricts NDIS drivers, including NDIS-WDM, from calling any other HAL macro or routine. If an NDIS driver calls any other HAL macro or routine, WHQL fails that driver.

READ_PORT_UCHAR

READ_PORT_USHORT

READ_PORT_ULONG

READ_PORT_BUFFER_UCHAR

READ_PORT_BUFFER_USHORT

READ_PORT_BUFFER_ULONG

WRITE_PORT_UCHAR

WRITE_PORT_USHORT

WRITE_PORT_ULONG

WRITE_PORT_BUFFER_UCHAR

WRITE_PORT_BUFFER_USHORT

WRITE_PORT_BUFFER_ULONG
