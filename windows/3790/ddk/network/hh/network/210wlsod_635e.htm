Pinpoint Wireless OIDs

The Pinpoint network and its associated technology are no longer available or in service. Therefore, Pinpoint-specific wireless OIDs are obsolete and should not be used. Definitions for these OIDs are still included for informational and historical purposes.

The following chart summarizes Pinpoint-specific wireless OIDs.

Q S Indication Name
M     OID_WW_PIN_LOC_AUTHORIZE
M     OID_WW_PIN_LAST_LOCATION
M     OID_WW_PIN_LOC_FIX
