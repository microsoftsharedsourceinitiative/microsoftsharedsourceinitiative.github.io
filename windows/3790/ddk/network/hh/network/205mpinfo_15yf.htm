Occasions for Setting Miniport Driver Information

Besides being called during initialization, the MiniportSetInformation function in a connectionless miniport driver or the MiniportCoRequest function in a connection-oriented miniport driver is called in two additional cases:

  • during a hardware reset, and
  • as a result of a protocol call to NdisCloseAdapter

MiniportSetInformation or MiniportCoRequest is called during a hardware reset operation as described in Hardware Reset. In this case, MiniportSetInformation or MiniportCoRequest is called to reset the miniport driver to its initial state with respect to its addresses.

NDIS calls MiniportSetInformation or MiniportCoRequest when a miniport driver’s NIC is closed by a protocol’s NdisCloseAdapter call. Such a miniport driver will be requested to update its addressing information.
