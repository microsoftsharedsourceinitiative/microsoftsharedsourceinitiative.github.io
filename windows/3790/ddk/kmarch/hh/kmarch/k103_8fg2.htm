HalSetBusData

The HalSetBusData routine is obsolete and is exported only to support existing drivers. Use the PnP Manager's IRP_MN_QUERY_INTERFACE and IRP_MN_WRITE_CONFIG requests instead.

ULONG 
  HalSetBusData(
    IN BUS_DATA_TYPE  BusDataType,
    IN ULONG  BusNumber,
    IN ULONG  SlotNumber,
    IN PVOID  Buffer,
    IN ULONG  Length
    );
