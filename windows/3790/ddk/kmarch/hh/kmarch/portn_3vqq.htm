Power Management Support Routines

All drivers that support power management call PoXxx routines. These routines are declared in the ntddk.h and wdm.h header files.

This section describes the following power management routines in alphabetical order:

PoCallDriver

PoRegisterDeviceForIdleDetection

PoRegisterSystemState

PoRequestPowerIrp

PoSetDeviceBusy

PoSetPowerState

PoSetSystemState

PoStartNextPowerIrp

PoUnregisterSystemState
