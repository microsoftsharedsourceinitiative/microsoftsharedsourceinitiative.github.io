VJOYD_JoystickActivity_Service

The VJOYD_JoystickActivity_Service macro is called internally to allow DirectInput to receive information about the joystick activity that is being reported through the WinMM functions. This information should prevent a screen saver from being activated. This macro should not be called by minidrivers and may not be supported in future versions.
