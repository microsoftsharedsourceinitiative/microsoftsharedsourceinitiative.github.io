W-Buffering DDI

The driver supports w-buffering by enabling the D3DPRASTERCAPS_WBUFFER cap in the dwRasterCaps member of the D3DPRIMCAPS structure. The D3DRENDERSTATE_ZENABLE render state is passed to the driver to enable or disable w-buffering or z-buffering.

The D3DHAL_DP2VIEWPORTINFO structure supports fields that correspond to world-space front and back clip planes (hither and yon respectively). This information can be used to adjust fog tables as well.
