Loading Highlight Data

The DXVA_Highlight structure specifies a highlighted rectangular area of a subpicture, and is used along with DCCMD data and a DPXD surface to create an alpha-blending surface. The highlight data is formatted in a manner compatible with the DVD ROM specification. For further clarification of DVD subpicture definition and data field interpretation, see DVD Specifications for Read-Only Disk: Part 3 - Video Specification (v. 1.11, May 1999).
